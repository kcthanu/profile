Dr. K. Chidambarathanu M.E., Ph.D.,


About Me

I am currently working as an Associate Professor in Department of Computer Science and Business Systems at R.M.K Engineering College, since June 2003. I have more than 20 years of experience in teaching and more than 8 years in research. I have obtained my B.E. (Computer Science and Engineering) from Madurai Kamaraj University, M.E. (Computer Science and Engineering) from Anna University, Chennai and Doctoral degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Anna University, Chennai. My area of interest includes Data Analytics, Web Programming and IoT. I have published papers in refereed journals, presented papers in international conferences and organized AICTE sponsored STTPs, workshops and FDPs. I have received Best Faculty Award from Cognizant and Virtusa. I am also a Wipro Certified Faculty in J2EE domain. I have attended many workshops and FDPs sponsored by AICTE and Anna University.
